The prestigious national Karachi Grammar STEM Olympiad (KGSO), organised by the Karachi Grammar School College Section, is back and bigger than ever.
On January 25th, 26th, and 27th 2024, be prepared for intense competition in our ten modules against hundreds of the fiercest participants from all over Pakistan, in one of its largest STEM competitions. From solving incredibly challenging maths and logic problems, to investigating an intricate murder mystery and facing off in famous virtual games, KGSO 2024 provides a challenge suitable for everyone.

Modules

Daedalus’ Downfall

Crime Module

Daedalus’ Downfall invites participants to put themselves in the shoes of a crime investigator. Participating teams will compete against each other to follow a breadcrumb trail and trace it back to the murderer. Furthermore, ciphers, morsels of evidence, and red herrings will be thrown in along the way, beckoning the teams to delve deeper into the mystery - all whilst racing against time. The race will end with a courtroom-style finale, where each team will have to debate in favour of prosecution of the suspect they think is guilty.
Only one team can perfectly fit all the pieces of the puzzle together, and ultimately solve the murder.

Synaptic Fusion

Biochem Module

Explore the fascinating realm where biology and chemistry converge! Delve into the molecular intricacies that govern life, unraveling the secrets of DNA, proteins, and cellular processes. Through engaging rounds and hands-on experiments, participants will unlock the mysteries of biomolecules, gaining a deeper understanding of how chemical principles shape the foundations of life. Join us on this scientific journey, where you'll not only expand your knowledge but also discover the profound connections between the microscopic world and the marvels of living organisms. Embrace the synergy of biology and chemistry in an enriching learning experience – your gateway to the captivating world of biochemistry awaits!
